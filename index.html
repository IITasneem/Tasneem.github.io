I'm Tasneem, a passionate painter and creative designer based in Saudi. My work revolves around graphic design, digital illustration, realistic painting, and abstract art. I strive to transform ideas and emotions into captivating visual art.

My Vision

I believe that art is a universal language that transcends barriers. Through my work, I aim to provoke thoughts and share cultural stories, Most of my inspiration comes from the Arab culture, and my personal experiences.
